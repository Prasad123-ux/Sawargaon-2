Population of Sawargaon:

Sawargaon is a large village located inHadgaon Taluka of Nanded district, Maharashtra with total 738 families residing. The Sawargaon village has population of 3709 of which 1871 are males while 1838 are females as per Population Census 2011.

In Sawargaon village population of children with age 0-6 is 557 which makes up 15.02 % of total population of village. Average Sex Ratio of Sawargaon village is 982 which is higher than Maharashtra state average of 929. Child Sex Ratio for the Sawargaon as per census is 948, higher than Maharashtra average of 894.

Sawargaon village has lower literacy rate compared to Maharashtra. In 2011, literacy rate of Sawargaon village was 72.11 % compared to 82.34 % of Maharashtra. In Sawargaon Male literacy stands at 81.70 % while female literacy rate was 62.41 %.
